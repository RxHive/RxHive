# Alfacalcidol 0.25mcg

**Generic Name:** Alfacalcidol

**Usage:** Alfacalcidol 0.25mcg is a synthetic vitamin D3 analogue used to treat various conditions related to vitamin D deficiency, including:

* **Hypoparathyroidism:** A condition where the parathyroid glands don't produce enough parathyroid hormone (PTH), leading to low blood calcium levels.
* **Pseudohypoparathyroidism:**  A condition where the body's tissues don't respond properly to PTH, resulting in low blood calcium levels.
* **Vitamin D-dependent rickets type I:** A rare inherited disorder where the kidneys don't properly activate vitamin D.
* **Osteomalacia:** Softening of the bones due to inadequate mineralization.  (Often used in conjunction with other treatments).
* **Renal osteodystrophy:** Bone disease associated with chronic kidney disease. (Often used in conjunction with other treatments).
