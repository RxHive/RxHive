**How it Works:** Acyclovir is a nucleoside analogue that inhibits viral DNA polymerase.  Essentially, it mimics a building block of viral DNA, preventing the virus from replicating its genetic material and thus stopping its spread.  It's more effective against actively replicating viruses.
