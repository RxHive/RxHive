**How it Works:**

* **Clotrimazole:** This is an azole antifungal agent. It works by interfering with the synthesis of ergosterol, a vital component of fungal cell membranes. This disruption leads to cell death and inhibits the growth of the fungus.

* **Allantoin:** This is a naturally occurring compound with soothing and healing properties. It helps to reduce inflammation, promote cell regeneration, and accelerate the healing process of damaged skin.  It doesn't directly treat the fungal infection but aids in skin recovery.

The combination leverages the antifungal action of clotrimazole and the skin-soothing effects of allantoin for more effective treatment and faster recovery.
