# Aliskiren 300mg + Hydrochlorothiazide 12.5mg

**How it Works:**

* **Aliskiren:** This drug inhibits renin, an enzyme crucial in the body's regulation of blood pressure.  By blocking renin, it reduces the production of angiotensin II, a hormone that narrows blood vessels, thus lowering blood pressure.

* **Hydrochlorothiazide:** This is a diuretic (water pill) that helps the body get rid of excess sodium and water. By reducing fluid volume in the body, it helps lower blood pressure.

The combination of these two drugs provides a synergistic effect, leading to a greater reduction in blood pressure than either drug alone.
