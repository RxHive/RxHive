# Alfacalcidol 1mcg

**Generic Name:** Alfacalcidol

**Usage:** Alfacalcidol is a synthetic vitamin D3 analogue used to treat conditions caused by vitamin D deficiency or impaired calcium metabolism.  Its primary uses include:

* **Hypoparathyroidism:**  A condition where the parathyroid glands don't produce enough parathyroid hormone (PTH), leading to low blood calcium levels.
* **Vitamin D-dependent rickets Type I:** A rare inherited disorder where the kidneys can't properly activate vitamin D.
* **Vitamin D-resistant rickets:**  A condition where the body doesn't respond properly to vitamin D.
* **Osteomalacia:** Softening of the bones due to vitamin D deficiency.
* **Secondary hyperparathyroidism:**  Elevated PTH levels due to chronic kidney disease or other conditions.
* **Hypocalcaemia:** Low levels of calcium in the blood. (Often used in conjunction with other treatments)

**Note:**  The specific usage and dosage will be determined by a doctor based on individual needs and condition.  Self-medicating with alfacalcidol is dangerous.
