# Abemaciclib 200mg

**Generic Name:** Abemaciclib

**Usage:** Abemaciclib is used in the treatment of certain types of breast cancer.  Specifically, it's indicated for:

* **HR-positive, HER2-negative advanced or metastatic breast cancer:**  In combination with an aromatase inhibitor (like letrozole or anastrozole) as first-line therapy (initial treatment) for patients with hormone receptor-positive (HR+), human epidermal growth factor receptor 2-negative (HER2-) disease.
* **HR-positive, HER2-negative advanced or metastatic breast cancer:**  As monotherapy (single drug treatment) for patients whose disease has progressed after endocrine therapy.

In simpler terms, it works by blocking the action of CDK4 and CDK6 enzymes, which are involved in the cell cycle and contribute to the growth and spread of cancer cells. By inhibiting these enzymes, abemaciclib slows down or stops the growth of cancer cells, particularly those that are hormone-receptor positive.
