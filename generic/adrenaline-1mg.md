**Usage:** Epinephrine 1mg is used in emergency situations to treat life-threatening allergic reactions (anaphylaxis), severe asthma attacks, and cardiac arrest. **How it Works:** Epinephrine acts on multiple receptor sites in the body, primarily alpha and beta adrenergic receptors.  It's a potent sympathomimetic amine that mimics the effects of the sympathetic nervous system. This leads to:
