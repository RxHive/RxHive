**How it Works:**

* **Activated Charcoal:**  A highly porous substance, activated charcoal works by adsorbing (binding to its surface) gases, toxins, and other substances in the digestive tract.  This helps to reduce the amount of gas causing bloating and discomfort. * **Simethicone:** This is an anti-foaming agent. It works by reducing the surface tension of gas bubbles in the intestines. This allows smaller gas bubbles to merge into larger ones, making it easier for them to pass through the digestive system and be expelled.
