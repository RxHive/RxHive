# Acipimox 250mg

**Generic Name:** Acipimox

**Usage:** Acipimox is used to lower triglyceride levels in the blood.  It's primarily used for the treatment of hypertriglyceridemia (high triglyceride levels), often in conjunction with lifestyle modifications like diet and exercise.  It's usually prescribed when other measures haven't been successful in lowering triglycerides to a safe level.  Prescribers will consider factors like other health conditions and medications the patient is taking when determining appropriateness.  It's *not* used to treat high cholesterol (high LDL or HDL levels).

**How it Works:** Acipimox is a nicotinic acid derivative. It works by increasing the activity of lipoprotein lipase (LPL), an enzyme that breaks down triglycerides in the blood. This leads to a reduction in triglyceride levels.  It's thought to also modestly affect other lipid parameters.  The exact mechanisms aren't fully understood.
