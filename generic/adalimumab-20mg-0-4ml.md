**Generic Name:** Adalimumab

**Usage:** Adalimumab is a tumor necrosis factor (TNF) blocker used to treat various inflammatory conditions.  Specific uses include:

* **Ankylosing spondylitis:** Reduces pain and inflammation in the spine.
* **Psoriasis:** Clears skin plaques and improves skin appearance.
* **Crohn's disease:** Reduces inflammation in the digestive tract.
* **Ulcerative colitis:** Reduces inflammation in the colon.
* **Hidradenitis suppurativa:** Reduces the number and severity of skin lesions.
* **Juvenile idiopathic arthritis:**  Reduces signs and symptoms in children.
* **Uveitis:** Reduces inflammation in the eye.

* **Rare but Serious:**  Serious infections, lymphoma and other cancers, demyelinating diseases (multiple sclerosis).

By neutralizing TNF-alpha, adalimumab reduces the inflammatory response in the body, thus alleviating the symptoms of various autoimmune and inflammatory diseases.
