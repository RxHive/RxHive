# Aceclofenac 100mg

**Generic Name:** Aceclofenac

**Usage:** Aceclofenac is a nonsteroidal anti-inflammatory drug (NSAID) used to relieve pain and inflammation associated with various conditions, including:

* Osteoarthritis

* Ankylosing spondylitis
* Acute pain (e.g., post-surgical pain, dental pain, menstrual cramps)
* Soft tissue injuries

* **Hepatic:** Liver problems (rare but possible).
* **Allergic reactions:**  These can range from mild skin reactions to severe anaphylaxis (rare but potentially life-threatening).


**How it Works:** Aceclofenac works by inhibiting the production of prostaglandins, which are substances in the body that cause inflammation, pain, and fever.  By reducing prostaglandin production, aceclofenac helps to alleviate pain and inflammation.
