# Acetylcysteine 1200mg

**Generic Name:** Acetylcysteine

**Usage:**

Acetylcysteine 1200mg is primarily used for:

* **Acute acetaminophen (paracetamol) overdose:** This is its most critical application.  It works to prevent liver damage caused by an overdose of acetaminophen. * **Chronic obstructive pulmonary disease (COPD):** It's used as a mucolytic agent to help thin and loosen mucus in the lungs, making it easier to cough up. This can improve breathing in individuals with conditions like bronchitis and emphysema.

* **Cystic fibrosis:** Similar to its use in COPD, it helps to break down mucus and improve lung function.

* **Other respiratory conditions:** It may be used to treat other conditions with excessive mucus buildup.

**How it Works:**

Acetylcysteine works through different mechanisms depending on the condition it's treating:

* **Acetaminophen overdose:** It acts as a precursor to glutathione, a vital antioxidant that helps the liver detoxify the harmful byproducts of acetaminophen metabolism. By replenishing glutathione levels, it protects the liver from damage.

* **Mucolytic agent:** It breaks down disulfide bonds in mucus, making it less viscous and easier to expel from the respiratory tract.  This improves airflow and reduces respiratory symptoms.
