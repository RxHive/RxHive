# Adapalene 0.1% w/w + Clindamycin 1.0% w/w

**Usage:** This combination medication is a topical treatment for acne vulgaris (acne).  It's used to treat inflammatory and non-inflammatory acne lesions (pimples, whiteheads, blackheads, and papules/pustules). Adapalene is a retinoid that helps prevent the formation of comedones (blockages in pores), while clindamycin is an antibiotic that helps reduce the bacteria (primarily *Cutibacterium acnes*) that contribute to acne inflammation.

**How it Works:**

* **Adapalene:** This retinoid works by:
    * Unblocking pores (comedones) by increasing cell turnover and shedding of dead skin cells.
    * Reducing inflammation.
    * Preventing the formation of new comedones.

* **Clindamycin:** This antibiotic works by:
    * Killing *Cutibacterium acnes* bacteria, a primary contributor to acne inflammation.


The combination of adapalene and clindamycin provides a synergistic effect, addressing both the formation of comedones and the bacterial component of acne.
