# Acebrophylline 50mg

**Generic Name:** Acebrophylline

**Usage:**

Acebrophylline is a bronchodilator used to treat reversible airway obstruction in conditions like asthma and chronic obstructive pulmonary disease (COPD). It relaxes the muscles around the airways in the lungs, making it easier to breathe.  It's typically used for long-term control of symptoms, and not for immediate relief of an acute attack.  The specific dosage and frequency will depend on the individual's condition and response to treatment, as determined by a physician.

**How it Works:**

Acebrophylline is a methylxanthine derivative. It works by relaxing the smooth muscles in the airways of the lungs, thereby widening the airways and improving airflow.  It achieves this by inhibiting the enzyme phosphodiesterase, leading to increased levels of cyclic AMP (cAMP).  Increased cAMP causes relaxation of the airway smooth muscle.
