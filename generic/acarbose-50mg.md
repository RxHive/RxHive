# Acarbose 50mg

**Generic Name:** Acarbose

**Usage:**

Acarbose is used to treat type 2 diabetes. It works by slowing down the digestion of carbohydrates in the small intestine. This leads to a slower and smaller rise in blood sugar levels after meals.  It's usually used in combination with diet and exercise, and sometimes other diabetes medications.  It is *not* used to treat type 1 diabetes.

**How it Works:**

Acarbose is an alpha-glucosidase inhibitor.  Alpha-glucosidases are enzymes in the small intestine that break down complex carbohydrates (starches and sugars) into simpler sugars (glucose) that are then absorbed into the bloodstream. By inhibiting these enzymes, acarbose slows down the absorption of glucose, thus preventing a rapid spike in blood sugar after meals.
