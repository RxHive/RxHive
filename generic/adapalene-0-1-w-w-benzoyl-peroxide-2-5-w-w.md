# Adapalene 0.1% w/w + Benzoyl Peroxide 2.5% w/w

**Usage:**  This combination medication is a topical treatment for acne vulgaris (acne).  It's used to treat mild to moderate acne that includes pimples, blackheads, and whiteheads. * **Dryness:**  The skin can become quite dry, especially initially.
* **Erythema (redness):**  Increased redness in the treated area.

* **Severe skin irritation:**  If irritation becomes severe, discontinue use and consult your doctor.

* **Adapalene:**  A retinoid, which works by increasing skin cell turnover. This helps unclog pores, reducing the formation of blackheads and whiteheads. It also helps to reduce inflammation.

* **Benzoyl Peroxide:**  An antimicrobial agent that kills the bacteria *Cutibacterium acnes* (formerly *Propionibacterium acnes*), a key contributor to acne development. It also helps to reduce inflammation.
