# Acetylcysteine 600mg

**Generic Name:** Acetylcysteine

* **Treatment of acetaminophen (paracetamol) overdose:** This is its most crucial use, acting as an antidote to prevent liver damage. Dosage and administration in this context are strictly determined by a physician based on the severity and timing of the overdose.
* **Breaking down mucus:** It's used to loosen and thin thick mucus in the respiratory tract, aiding in its expectoration. This is helpful in conditions like chronic obstructive pulmonary disease (COPD), cystic fibrosis, and bronchitis.

**How it Works:**

Acetylcysteine works through multiple mechanisms depending on its use:

* **In acetaminophen overdose:** It acts as a glutathione precursor. Glutathione is an essential antioxidant that helps detoxify the toxic metabolite of acetaminophen (N-acetyl-p-benzoquinone imine, NAPQI), preventing liver damage.
* **As a mucolytic:** It breaks disulfide bonds in mucus glycoproteins, reducing the viscosity of mucus and making it easier to cough up.
