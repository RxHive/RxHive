The combination increases effectiveness and reduces the likelihood of drug resistance developing. This combination works by targeting different stages of *Mycobacterium tuberculosis* growth and replication:

* **Rifampicin:** Inhibits bacterial RNA synthesis, preventing protein production and bacterial replication.
* **Isoniazid:** Interferes with mycolic acid synthesis, essential for the bacterial cell wall.
* **Pyrazinamide:** Its exact mechanism is not fully understood, but it's believed to affect bacterial metabolism, particularly at acidic pH levels found in macrophages.
* **Ethambutol:** Inhibits arabinosyl transferases, which are crucial for the synthesis of arabinogalactan, a key component of the mycobacterial cell wall.
