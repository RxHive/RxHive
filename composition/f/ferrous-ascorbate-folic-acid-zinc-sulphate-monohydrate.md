# Ferrous Ascorbate + Folic Acid + Zinc Sulphate Monohydrate

**Generic Name:** Ferrous Ascorbate + Folic Acid + Zinc Sulphate Monohydrate

**Usage:** This combination is primarily used as a dietary supplement to treat or prevent deficiencies in iron, folate (folic acid), and zinc.  Specific uses may include:

* **Iron deficiency anemia:**  Iron is crucial for red blood cell production.  Ferrous ascorbate is a form of iron that's relatively well-absorbed.
* **Folate deficiency:** Folate is essential for cell growth and development, particularly important during pregnancy to prevent neural tube defects.
* **Zinc deficiency:** Zinc plays a vital role in many bodily functions, including immune function, wound healing, and cell growth.

It's important to note that this supplement should be taken only under the guidance of a healthcare professional, especially if you have pre-existing conditions or are taking other medications.  Self-treating deficiencies can be risky. The dosage will depend on the individual's specific needs and deficiency levels.

**Side Effects:** While generally well-tolerated, side effects can occur, and their severity varies depending on the dosage and individual sensitivity. These may include:

* **Gastrointestinal issues:** Nausea, constipation, diarrhea, stomach upset, heartburn. These are more common with iron supplementation.
* **Allergic reactions:**  Rare but possible, especially with any of the individual components.  Symptoms can range from mild rash to severe anaphylaxis.
* **Other potential side effects:**  Headache, dizziness, dark stools (from iron), metallic taste in the mouth.

**How it Works:**

* **Ferrous Ascorbate:** Provides iron in a readily absorbable form. Iron is a component of hemoglobin, which carries oxygen throughout the body.  Ascorbic acid (vitamin C) enhances iron absorption.
* **Folic Acid:** A synthetic form of folate (vitamin B9), crucial for DNA synthesis and cell division.  It plays a vital role in red blood cell formation and prevents neural tube defects in developing fetuses.
* **Zinc Sulphate Monohydrate:** Provides zinc, a mineral involved in numerous enzymatic processes and essential for immune function, wound healing, and cell growth.
