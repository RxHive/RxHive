# Xylometazoline

**Generic Name:** Xylometazoline

**Usage:** Xylometazoline is a nasal decongestant used to temporarily relieve stuffiness and congestion in the nose caused by the common cold, hay fever, or other allergies. * **Sneezing:**  This is sometimes experienced after application.

* **Insomnia (difficulty sleeping):** Especially if used later in the day.

**How it Works:** Xylometazoline is a sympathomimetic amine.  It works by constricting the blood vessels in the nasal passages. This reduces swelling and inflammation, allowing for easier breathing.
