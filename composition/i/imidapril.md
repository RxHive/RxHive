# Imidapril

**Generic Name:** Imidapril

**Usage:** Imidapril is an angiotensin-converting enzyme (ACE) inhibitor used to treat high blood pressure (hypertension) and heart failure.  It may also be used to reduce the risk of stroke in patients with high blood pressure and left ventricular hypertrophy.  The specific dosage and regimen will be determined by a physician based on individual patient needs and response.

**How it Works:** Imidapril works by inhibiting the angiotensin-converting enzyme (ACE). ACE is responsible for converting angiotensin I to angiotensin II, a potent vasoconstrictor (a substance that narrows blood vessels). By inhibiting ACE, imidapril reduces the production of angiotensin II, leading to vasodilation (widening of blood vessels), reduced blood pressure, and decreased strain on the heart. This mechanism also contributes to its effectiveness in treating heart failure.
