### Usage

This combination medication is primarily used for the relief of heartburn, acid indigestion, and upset stomach associated with excess stomach acid.  The three components work synergistically:

* **Oxetacaine:** A local anesthetic that numbs the irritated tissues in the esophagus and stomach, providing rapid relief from pain and burning sensations.
* **Aluminium Hydroxide:** An antacid that neutralizes stomach acid, reducing its overall acidity.
* **Milk of Magnesia (Magnesium Hydroxide):** Another antacid that also acts as a mild laxative. This can help relieve constipation that might be associated with certain antacid use.

The combination is designed to provide both rapid pain relief (via oxetacaine) and longer-term acid neutralization (via the antacids), addressing both the symptoms and contributing causes of heartburn and indigestion.  It is generally for short-term symptom relief rather than long-term acid management.

### Side Effects

Common side effects may include:

* Constipation
* Diarrhea
* Nausea
* Vomiting
* Stomach pain
* Dizziness
* Headache

Less common, but potentially serious side effects include:

* Allergic reactions (rash, itching, swelling, difficulty breathing)
* Magnesium toxicity (seen with excessive intake of Milk of Magnesia, especially in individuals with kidney problems)
* Aluminum toxicity (less common but can occur with long-term, high-dose use)

**If you experience any serious or concerning side effects, discontinue use and seek immediate medical advice.**


### How it Works

As mentioned earlier, this is a combination medication:

* **Oxetacaine** works by blocking nerve signals in the affected area, thus reducing the sensation of pain and burning.
* **Aluminium Hydroxide** chemically neutralizes stomach acid (hydrochloric acid) by reacting with it, forming a less irritating salt.
* **Milk of Magnesia** also neutralizes stomach acid and possesses mild laxative properties, due to its magnesium content, which increases water in the bowel, promoting easier bowel movements.  This counteracts the potential constipation caused by aluminum-based antacids.


### Precautions

* **Contraindications:** This medication should not be used if you have known allergies to any of the ingredients.  Individuals with kidney disease should exercise extreme caution with Milk of Magnesia due to the risk of magnesium toxicity.  Pregnant or breastfeeding women should consult their doctor before use.
* **Drug Interactions:** This medication may interact with other drugs, such as certain antibiotics and diuretics. Inform your doctor or pharmacist about all medications you are currently taking.
* **Warnings:**  Prolonged use without consulting a doctor is not recommended.  If symptoms persist or worsen, consult a healthcare professional.  This medication is not intended for long-term use; it's primarily for temporary relief of symptoms.
