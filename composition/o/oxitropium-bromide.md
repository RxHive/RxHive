# Oxitropium Bromide

Oxitropium Bromide

### Usage
Oxitropium bromide is a bronchodilator, a type of medication that helps open up the airways in the lungs.  It was primarily used to treat reversible airway obstruction, a condition where the airways narrow and make breathing difficult. This includes conditions such as asthma and chronic obstructive pulmonary disease (COPD). ### How it Works
Oxitropium bromide works by blocking muscarinic receptors (specifically M-receptors) in the airways. These receptors are part of the parasympathetic nervous system, which normally causes the airways to constrict and increases mucus production. By blocking these receptors, oxitropium bromide prevents this constriction, allowing the airways to relax and widen, making breathing easier.  It also reduces mucus secretion, further improving airflow.
