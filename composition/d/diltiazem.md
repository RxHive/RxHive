# Diltiazem

**Generic Name:** Diltiazem

**Usage:** Diltiazem is a calcium channel blocker used to treat several conditions, including:

* **High blood pressure (hypertension):**  It helps relax blood vessels, making it easier for the heart to pump blood.
* **Chest pain (angina):** It reduces the workload on the heart by widening blood vessels, increasing blood flow to the heart muscle.
* **Certain types of irregular heartbeat (atrial fibrillation, atrial flutter):** It helps to slow down the heart rate and restore a more normal rhythm.
* **Prinzmetal's angina (variant angina):** A specific type of angina caused by spasms in the coronary arteries.  Diltiazem helps prevent these spasms.

**Note:** The specific dosage and frequency of Diltiazem will vary depending on the condition being treated and the individual's health status.  Always follow your doctor's instructions.


**Side Effects:**  Like all medications, Diltiazem can cause side effects.  These can vary in severity and frequency. Common side effects include:

* **Headache:** A very common side effect.
* **Dizziness:** Can be caused by a drop in blood pressure.
* **Nausea:**  A feeling of sickness.
* **Fatigue:** Feeling tired or weak.
* **Swelling in the ankles or feet:** Due to fluid retention.
* **Constipation:**  A common gastrointestinal side effect.
* **Heartburn:**  A burning sensation in the chest.
* **Low blood pressure (hypotension):**  This can cause lightheadedness or fainting.


Less common but more serious side effects include:

* **Irregular heartbeat:** Can be a serious side effect.
* **Liver problems:**  Symptoms include jaundice (yellowing of the skin and eyes).
* **Heart failure:**  Symptoms include shortness of breath and swelling in the legs and feet.
* **Severe allergic reactions:**  Symptoms can include difficulty breathing, swelling of the face, lips, or tongue.


**How it Works:** Diltiazem works by blocking calcium channels in the heart and blood vessels. Calcium is important for the contraction of heart muscle and the constriction of blood vessels. By blocking calcium channels, Diltiazem:

* **Relaxes blood vessels:** This lowers blood pressure and improves blood flow to the heart.
* **Slows the heart rate:** This reduces the workload on the heart.
* **Reduces the force of heart contractions:** This also helps to reduce the workload on the heart.
