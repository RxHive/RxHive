# Dinoprost
### Generic Name
Dinoprost

### Usage
Dinoprost is a synthetic prostaglandin F2 alpha analog primarily used for the termination of pregnancy, particularly in cases of missed miscarriage, hydatidiform mole, and intrauterine fetal death.  While it has been used in the past to induce labor, its higher incidence of adverse effects compared to dinoprostone has led to its discontinuation for this purpose in most regions.  It's important to note that Dinoprost is currently discontinued in most countries.

### How it Works
Dinoprost works by mimicking the actions of prostaglandin F2 alpha. It binds to specific receptors in the uterine muscle (myometrium), causing strong contractions.  These contractions are similar to those experienced during labor and are potent enough to induce an abortion or expel the uterine contents.  Furthermore, Dinoprost assists in softening and dilating the cervix, facilitating the process.
