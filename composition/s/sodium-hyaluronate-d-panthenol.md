# Sodium Hyaluronate + D-Panthenol

**Generic Name:** Sodium Hyaluronate + D-Panthenol

**Usage:**

Sodium Hyaluronate and D-Panthenol are often used in combination in topical skincare products, such as creams, serums, and lotions.  Their combined action targets hydration and skin repair. Specific uses include:

* **Hydration:** Sodium hyaluronate (hyaluronic acid) attracts and retains moisture in the skin, improving hydration and plumpness.
* **Wound Healing:** D-panthenol (provitamin B5) promotes cell regeneration and wound healing, reducing inflammation and soothing irritated skin.
* **Skin Barrier Repair:** Both ingredients contribute to strengthening the skin's protective barrier, making it less susceptible to dryness, irritation, and environmental damage.
* **Treatment of Dry Skin:**  The combination is effective in treating various forms of dry skin, including xerosis (dry skin), eczema (in some cases, as an adjunct therapy), and minor burns.

**Side Effects:**

Generally, Sodium Hyaluronate and D-Panthenol are well-tolerated with minimal side effects.  However, some individuals may experience:

* **Mild irritation:**  Redness, itching, or burning sensation at the application site, especially if sensitive skin is involved.
* **Allergic reactions:** Although rare, allergic reactions are possible.  These can manifest as rash, hives, or swelling.  Discontinue use if an allergic reaction occurs.


**How it Works:**

* **Sodium Hyaluronate:** This is a naturally occurring substance found in the body.  It acts as a humectant, meaning it draws moisture from the air and binds it to the skin.  This increases hydration and improves skin elasticity.  It also has some anti-inflammatory properties.

* **D-Panthenol:** This is a provitamin, meaning it converts to pantothenic acid (vitamin B5) in the skin.  Pantothenic acid is crucial for cell growth and repair.  D-panthenol promotes skin regeneration, reduces inflammation, and soothes irritated skin.  It also helps to improve the skin barrier function.

The combined action of these two ingredients leads to synergistic effects, providing superior hydration, skin repair, and protection compared to using either ingredient alone.
