### Side Effects

Possible side effects can vary depending on the concentration of the ingredients and individual sensitivity.  Some possible side effects include:

* **Skin irritation:**  Redness, burning, itching, or swelling at the application site.
* **Allergic reactions:**  Rare but possible, especially with aloe vera or other added ingredients. Symptoms may include rash, hives, itching, swelling, and difficulty breathing.
* **Skin discoloration (silver staining):** Silver nitrate can cause temporary or, in rare cases, permanent discoloration of the skin, particularly with prolonged or high-concentration use.
* **Chlorhexidine-related side effects:**  While generally well-tolerated, chlorhexidine can occasionally cause mild stinging or burning sensation.


**Always consult a healthcare provider if you experience any adverse effects.**
