**How it Works:**  Each drug works through a different mechanism to target specific aspects of cardiovascular health:

* **Simvastatin:** Inhibits cholesterol production in the liver.
* **Ramipril:** Blocks the action of angiotensin-converting enzyme (ACE), reducing blood pressure and improving blood vessel function.
* **Atenolol:** Blocks the effects of adrenaline on the heart, reducing heart rate and blood pressure.
* **Hydrochlorothiazide:** Increases the excretion of sodium and water from the body, thereby lowering blood pressure.
* **Aspirin:** Inhibits platelet aggregation, preventing blood clot formation.
