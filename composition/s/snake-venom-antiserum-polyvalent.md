# Snake Venom Antiserum Polyvalent

It is administered intravenously (IV) by a medical professional in a hospital or other equipped medical facility.  Treatment should begin as soon as possible after the bite. These antibodies specifically bind to and neutralize the toxins (venom components) in the snake venom, preventing them from causing further damage to the body's tissues and organs.  Essentially, the antibodies "mop up" the venom.
