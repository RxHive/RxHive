# Sodium Stibogluconate

**Generic Name:** Sodium Stibogluconate

**Usage:** Sodium stibogluconate is an antimonial drug primarily used to treat visceral leishmaniasis (kala azar), a parasitic disease caused by *Leishmania donovani*.  It's also sometimes used, though less commonly and often with other treatments, for cutaneous leishmaniasis (skin lesions) and mucocutaneous leishmaniasis (lesions affecting the mucous membranes).  The specific formulation and dosage will be determined by a physician based on the patient's condition and the type of leishmaniasis.
