# Satranidazole

**Generic Name:** Satranidazole

**Usage:** Satranidazole is an antiprotozoal medication primarily used to treat infections caused by certain parasites.  Its most common use is in the treatment of:

* **Amoebiasis:**  Specifically, intestinal amoebiasis (caused by *Entamoeba histolytica*) and amoebic liver abscess.
* **Giardiasis:** Infection of the small intestine caused by *Giardia lamblia*.
* **Trichomoniasis:** A sexually transmitted infection caused by *Trichomonas vaginalis*.
