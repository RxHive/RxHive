### Side Effects
The potential side effects of ingesting silver colloid are significant and can be severe.  They include:

* **Argyria:** This is the most serious side effect, causing a permanent bluish-gray discoloration of the skin, mucous membranes, and internal organs due to the accumulation of silver in the body.  This is irreversible.
* **Gastrointestinal issues:** Nausea, vomiting, diarrhea.
* **Kidney problems:**  Silver can damage the kidneys.
* **Neurological effects:**  Although rare, some reports suggest potential neurological side effects.
* **Drug interactions:**  Silver colloid may interact with medications, though the specifics of these interactions are largely unknown due to a lack of research.

It is vital to seek immediate medical attention if any adverse effects occur after using silver colloid.

### How it Works
The purported mechanism of action for silver colloid is based on the idea that silver ions can interfere with the metabolic processes of microorganisms, inhibiting their growth and potentially killing them. However, the concentration of silver ions needed to achieve this effect in the body is far higher than what is typically found in commercially available silver colloid products and carries significant risk of toxicity.  The lack of well-designed studies makes it impossible to confirm whether this mechanism is actually effective or safe in a clinical setting.
