# Stannous fluoride

**Generic Name:** Stannous fluoride

**Usage:**

Stannous fluoride is primarily used as an active ingredient in some toothpastes and mouthwashes to prevent tooth decay (dental caries).  It works by strengthening tooth enamel and inhibiting the growth of bacteria that contribute to cavities.  It's also sometimes used in dental treatments applied directly by a dentist, such as fluoride gels or varnishes, to further protect teeth.  The concentration in over-the-counter products is generally lower than in professional applications.

* **Discoloration of teeth:** This is the most commonly reported side effect, often appearing as a slight yellowing or staining, especially on teeth with existing stains or imperfections.  This is usually reversible with professional cleaning.
* **Metallic taste:** Some users report a temporary metallic taste in their mouth.
* **Irritation of the mouth or gums:**  Rarely, some individuals may experience mild irritation of the gums or mouth lining.  This is usually temporary and resolves with discontinued use.
* **Gastrointestinal upset (if ingested):**  Ingestion of large quantities could lead to nausea or stomach upset.  However, this is unlikely with proper use of toothpaste and mouthwash.
