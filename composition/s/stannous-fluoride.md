# Stannous Fluoride
### Generic Name
Stannous Fluoride

### Usage
Stannous fluoride is primarily used for the prevention of dental caries (tooth decay). It works topically on the teeth to enhance their resistance to acid erosion, promote the remineralization of weakened enamel, and hinder the activity of bacteria that contribute to cavities.  While its primary use is preventative, it can also play a supportive role in managing existing dental caries by improving tooth structure and reducing the risk of further decay.


### Dosage

**Adult Dosage:**  For caries prevention, stannous fluoride is typically applied topically.  Two common forms exist:

* **Rinse (1% solution):** A 15 mL rinse of a diluted 0.1% solution is used once daily for one minute, followed by expectoration (spitting out).
* **Paste (0.4%):** An appropriate amount of 0.4% paste is applied to a toothbrush and used twice daily.  Brush thoroughly, ensuring the paste remains on teeth for at least one minute before spitting out. Avoid rinsing, eating, or drinking for 30 minutes after application.


**Pediatric Dosage:**  For children over 12 years of age, the dosage is the same as for adults.  The safety and efficacy of stannous fluoride in children under 12 years of age have not been established.  Always consult a dentist or pediatrician before using any fluoride product in young children.


**Dosage Adjustments:**  The manufacturer's labeling does not provide specific dosage adjustments for individuals with hepatic (liver) or renal (kidney) impairment.  Consult a healthcare professional for guidance in these cases.


### Side Effects

* Allergic reactions (frequency not specified)


It's crucial to note that while serious side effects are rare, any unexpected reactions should be reported immediately to a healthcare provider.


### How it Works

Stannous fluoride's action is primarily topical. It works in several ways to protect teeth:

* **Remineralization:** It helps to replace lost minerals in the tooth enamel, strengthening weakened areas and making them more resistant to acid attacks.
* **Antibacterial Effect:** It inhibits the growth and activity of bacteria found in dental plaque, reducing the production of acids that cause tooth decay.
* **Acid Resistance:** It increases the resistance of tooth enamel to acids, making it less susceptible to demineralization.


### Precautions

* **Excessive Ingestion:**  Prolonged ingestion of excessive amounts of stannous fluoride can lead to dental fluorosis (mottling of the teeth) and changes in bone structure, especially if water fluoridation levels exceed 0.6 ppm.
* **Interactions:** While no specific drug interactions are widely reported, it's advisable to inform your dentist or physician about all medications and supplements you are taking.
* **Pregnancy and Lactation:** Stannous fluoride is categorized as Pregnancy Category B (animal reproduction studies have not shown risk, but there are no adequate and well-controlled studies in pregnant women).  Whether it's excreted in breast milk is unknown; therefore, use should be cautious during breastfeeding.
* **Dairy Products:** Avoid consuming dairy products immediately before or after applying stannous fluoride, as they may interfere with its absorption and effectiveness.
* **Children Under 12:** The safety and efficacy of stannous fluoride have not been established in children under 12 years of age.


### FAQs

* **Q: How often should I use stannous fluoride?**
    * **A:**  Follow the specific instructions provided by your dentist or as indicated on the product label. This typically involves daily or twice-daily application.

* **Q: What should I do if I accidentally swallow some stannous fluoride?**
    * **A:**  If a significant amount is swallowed, contact a poison control center or your doctor immediately.

* **Q: How should I store stannous fluoride?**
    * **A:** Store according to the instructions on the product label.  Generally, it should be kept in a cool, dry place, away from children.

* **Q: Can I use stannous fluoride if I have other dental issues?**
    * **A:**  Always consult your dentist before using stannous fluoride, especially if you have other dental conditions or are undergoing dental treatment.  They can assess your individual needs and provide appropriate guidance.

* **Q: Is stannous fluoride safe for everyone?**
    * **A:** While generally safe when used as directed, stannous fluoride may not be suitable for everyone.  Consult a dentist or healthcare professional for personalized advice, particularly if you have specific health concerns or are pregnant or breastfeeding.
