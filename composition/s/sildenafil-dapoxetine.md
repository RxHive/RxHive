# Sildenafil + Dapoxetine

**Generic Name:** Sildenafil + Dapoxetine

**Usage:** This combination medication is used to treat both erectile dysfunction (ED) and premature ejaculation (PE) in men.  Sildenafil helps achieve and maintain an erection, while dapoxetine delays ejaculation, allowing for longer sexual intercourse. It's important to note that this combination is not approved in all countries and may be prescribed off-label in some regions.  Always consult a doctor before use.

**Side Effects:**  Side effects can vary depending on the individual, but common ones include:

* **Headache:** Frequently reported.
* **Facial flushing:** Redness in the face.
* **Nasal congestion:** Runny or stuffy nose.
* **Dyspepsia (indigestion):** Upset stomach.
* **Diarrhea:** Loose stools.
* **Dizziness:** Feeling lightheaded or faint.
* **Visual disturbances:** Blurred vision, changes in color perception.
* **Back pain:**  Aching in the back.
* **Muscle aches:** Pain or soreness in the muscles.
* **Nausea:** Feeling sick to the stomach.
* **Sleep disturbances:** Difficulty sleeping or insomnia.

More serious, though less common, side effects include:

* **Priapism:** A prolonged and painful erection lasting more than 4 hours.  Seek immediate medical attention if this occurs.
* **Sudden vision loss:**  Stop use immediately and contact a doctor.
* **Hearing loss:**  Stop use immediately and contact a doctor.
* **Heart attack or stroke:** Individuals with pre-existing cardiovascular conditions are at increased risk.


**How it Works:**

* **Sildenafil:** Works by inhibiting the enzyme phosphodiesterase-5 (PDE5), which increases blood flow to the penis, facilitating an erection in response to sexual stimulation.  It does *not* cause an erection without sexual stimulation.
* **Dapoxetine:** Works by inhibiting the reuptake of serotonin, a neurotransmitter that plays a role in ejaculatory control. This delays the time it takes to reach orgasm, thus treating premature ejaculation.
