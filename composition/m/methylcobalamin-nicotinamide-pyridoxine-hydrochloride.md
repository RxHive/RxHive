# Methylcobalamin + Nicotinamide + Pyridoxine Hydrochloride

* **Nerve function support:**  All three vitamins play crucial roles in nerve health.  The combination may be used to improve nerve function in conditions like peripheral neuropathy.
* **Energy metabolism:** These B vitamins are essential coenzymes in various metabolic pathways that produce energy.  A deficiency can lead to fatigue; supplementation aims to improve energy levels.
* **Cognitive function:**  Some studies suggest these vitamins may support cognitive function, although more research is needed.
* **Treatment of specific deficiencies:**  It's often prescribed to treat diagnosed deficiencies in B12, B3, or B6, individually or in combination.
