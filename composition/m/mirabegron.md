# Mirabegron

**Generic Name:** Mirabegron

**Usage:**

Mirabegron is used to treat overactive bladder (OAB).  OAB is a condition characterized by urinary urgency, frequency, and urge incontinence (leakage of urine).  It's prescribed to help reduce these symptoms.  It is *not* indicated for the treatment of urinary tract infections (UTIs) or other bladder conditions.

**How it Works:**

Mirabegron is a beta-3 adrenergic receptor agonist.  This means it works by relaxing the muscles in the bladder.  Specifically, it selectively activates beta-3 adrenergic receptors in the detrusor muscle (the muscle that contracts to empty the bladder). By relaxing this muscle, it increases bladder capacity and reduces the frequency of bladder contractions, thus lessening the symptoms of overactive bladder.
