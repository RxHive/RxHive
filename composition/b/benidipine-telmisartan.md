# Benidipine + Telmisartan
### Generic Name
Benidipine + Telmisartan

### Usage
Benidipine + Telmisartan is a combination medication used to treat hypertension (high blood pressure).  It works by combining two different types of blood pressure-lowering medications: a calcium channel blocker (benidipine) and an angiotensin receptor blocker (telmisartan).  Benidipine relaxes blood vessels, making it easier for blood to flow, while telmisartan blocks the action of a hormone called angiotensin II, which narrows blood vessels.  By working together, these two drugs effectively lower blood pressure, reducing the risk of heart attack, stroke, and kidney disease. This combination is primarily used for patients whose blood pressure isn't adequately controlled with a single medication.  It's not typically a first-line treatment for hypertension.

### Dosage
Dosage of Benidipine + Telmisartan should always be determined by a healthcare professional and tailored to the individual patient's needs and response. There is no single universal dosage.  The medication usually comes in tablet form.   Dosage instructions will vary depending on the specific brand and the combination of benidipine and telmisartan strengths within the tablet.  

* **Adults:**  A typical starting dose might be one tablet once daily, but this can be adjusted based on blood pressure readings. The doctor will gradually increase the dose if needed, monitoring blood pressure closely.

* **Children:** This combination medication is not typically used in children. High blood pressure treatment in children requires different approaches.

* **Elderly:** Dosage adjustments might be necessary for older adults due to potential changes in kidney function.

* **IV Administration:** Benidipine + Telmisartan is not available in an injectable (IV) form.


**Important Note:**  Never adjust your dosage without consulting your doctor or pharmacist.  Always follow the prescribed dosage and instructions carefully.

### How it Works
Benidipine, a calcium channel blocker, works by relaxing the muscles in the walls of your blood vessels. This widening of blood vessels reduces resistance to blood flow, lowering blood pressure.  Telmisartan, an angiotensin receptor blocker (ARB), blocks the action of angiotensin II, a hormone that causes blood vessels to constrict. By preventing this constriction, telmisartan further helps lower blood pressure.  The combination of these two drugs provides a synergistic effect, leading to a more substantial reduction in blood pressure than either drug alone in many patients.

### Precautions
* **Contraindications:**  This medication is contraindicated in individuals with a known allergy to benidipine, telmisartan, or any of the inactive ingredients in the formulation. It should also be avoided in patients with severe liver disease.

* **Drug Interactions:** Benidipine + Telmisartan can interact with other medications, including potassium-sparing diuretics, ACE inhibitors, NSAIDs (nonsteroidal anti-inflammatory drugs), and other blood pressure medications.  Inform your doctor of all medications, supplements, and herbal remedies you are taking.

* **Pregnancy and Breastfeeding:** This medication is generally not recommended during pregnancy, especially in the second and third trimesters.  Consult your doctor before taking this medication if you are pregnant, breastfeeding, or planning to become pregnant.

* **Elderly:** Older adults may be more sensitive to the side effects of this medication. Careful monitoring of blood pressure and kidney function is important.

* **Kidney and Liver Disease:** Dosage adjustments may be necessary for patients with impaired kidney or liver function.


### FAQs

* **Q: Can I drink alcohol while taking Benidipine + Telmisartan?**  A: Alcohol can lower blood pressure and increase the risk of dizziness and fainting, especially when combined with this medication. Limit alcohol consumption or avoid it altogether while taking this medication.

* **Q: How should I store this medication?** A: Store this medication in a cool, dry place, away from direct sunlight and moisture, as instructed on the label. Keep it out of reach of children.

* **Q: What should I do if I miss a dose?** A: Take the missed dose as soon as you remember, unless it's almost time for your next dose.  Never double up on doses.

* **Q: Can I stop taking this medication suddenly?** A: No. Stopping this medication abruptly can lead to a sudden increase in blood pressure.  Always consult your doctor before stopping or changing your medication.

* **Q:  How long will it take to see results?** A:  It may take several weeks to see the full effects of this medication on your blood pressure. Regular monitoring is essential.
