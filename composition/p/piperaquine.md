# Piperaquine

**Generic Name:** Piperaquine

**Usage:** Piperaquine is an antimalarial drug used to treat malaria caused by *Plasmodium falciparum*, *Plasmodium vivax*, *Plasmodium ovale*, and *Plasmodium malariae*.  It's often used in combination with other antimalarial drugs, most commonly dihydroartemisinin (e.g., Artekinet, which combines piperaquine and dihydroartemisinin).  The specific usage and dosage will depend on the individual's age, weight, and the type of malaria.  It's crucial to follow a doctor's prescription precisely.  Piperaquine alone is rarely used due to the risk of resistance.
