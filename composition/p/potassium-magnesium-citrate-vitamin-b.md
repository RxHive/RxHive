## Potassium Magnesium Citrate + Vitamin B

**Usage:**  This combination is often used as a dietary supplement to address deficiencies in potassium, magnesium, and B vitamins.  Specific uses might include:

* **Muscle cramps and spasms:** Potassium and magnesium are crucial for muscle function.

* **Nerve function:** Both potassium and magnesium are essential for proper nerve function, and B vitamins support nerve health.
* **Heart health:**  Potassium and magnesium play a role in maintaining a healthy heart rhythm.

**Side Effects:** Side effects vary depending on the individual and dosage, and can include:
