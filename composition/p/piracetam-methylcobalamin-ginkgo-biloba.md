### Side Effects

Side effects can vary depending on individual sensitivity and the dosages of each component:

* **Piracetam:** Common side effects are usually mild and can include headache, insomnia, dizziness, anxiety, and nausea.  Rarely, more serious side effects are reported.
* **Methylcobalamin:** Side effects are rare and usually mild.  They can include diarrhea, nausea, vomiting, and skin reactions.  High doses can cause liver problems.
* **Ginkgo Biloba:**  Common side effects are mild and include headaches, upset stomach, and dizziness.  However, more serious side effects like allergic reactions are possible.

**Important:**  If you experience any unexpected or concerning side effects, discontinue use and seek medical attention immediately.



### How it Works

Each component in this combination acts differently, but the combined aim is to enhance cognitive function through various mechanisms:

* **Piracetam:** Thought to improve neuronal membrane fluidity, enhance acetylcholine neurotransmission, and improve cerebral blood flow.
* **Methylcobalamin:** Plays a vital role in myelin synthesis, protecting nerve cells and facilitating nerve signal transmission. Crucial for normal brain function.
* **Ginkgo Biloba:** Believed to enhance blood circulation to the brain, improve oxygen and nutrient delivery, and potentially increase the levels of neurotransmitters.


### Precautions

* **Interactions:** This combination may interact with other medications, such as blood thinners (warfarin) and antiplatelet drugs (aspirin). Consult your doctor about potential drug interactions.
* **Pregnancy and Breastfeeding:** The safety of this combination during pregnancy and breastfeeding is not fully established. Consult your doctor before using it.
* **Bleeding Disorders:** Ginkgo Biloba can increase the risk of bleeding.  Avoid use if you have bleeding disorders.
* **Seizures:**  While rare, piracetam in high doses may increase the risk of seizures.
* **Allergies:** Be aware of potential allergic reactions to any of the ingredients.
* **Liver and Kidney Function:**  Monitor these functions, especially with long-term use.
