# Praziquantel

**Generic Name:** Praziquantel

**Usage:**  Praziquantel is an antiparasitic drug used to treat infections caused by several types of flatworms (trematodes and cestodes).  Specific uses include:

* **Schistosomiasis:** A parasitic disease caused by *Schistosoma* worms. This is a major use of praziquantel.
* **Cysticercosis:** Caused by the larval stage of the pork tapeworm (*Taenia solium*).
* **Neurocysticercosis:** A serious form of cysticercosis affecting the brain and central nervous system.
* **Echinococcosis:** Caused by *Echinococcus* tapeworms. Treatment may be combined with surgery or other interventions.
* **Tapeworm infections:**  Various tapeworm infections, including those caused by *Taenia saginata* (beef tapeworm), *Taenia africana*, *Hymenolepis nana*, *Diphyllobothrium latum* (fish tapeworm), and others.

**Note:** Praziquantel is a prescription medication and should only be used under the guidance of a healthcare professional.  The specific dosage and treatment regimen will vary depending on the type of infection, the severity of the infection, the patient's age and weight, and other factors.
