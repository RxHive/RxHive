# Piroxicam + Beta-Cyclodextrin

**Generic Name:** Piroxicam-Beta-Cyclodextrin complex (Note:  This isn't a single, universally approved generic name. The specific name might vary depending on the manufacturer and country.)

**Usage:**

Piroxicam-Beta-Cyclodextrin is used to relieve the symptoms of osteoarthritis, rheumatoid arthritis, and ankylosing spondylitis.  The beta-cyclodextrin is included to improve the solubility and bioavailability of piroxicam, making it easier for the body to absorb and potentially reducing gastrointestinal side effects associated with piroxicam alone.  It's an NSAID (nonsteroidal anti-inflammatory drug).

**Side Effects:**

The side effects are similar to those of piroxicam alone, but potentially lessened due to the improved bioavailability profile provided by the beta-cyclodextrin.  These can include:

* **Gastrointestinal:**  Nausea, vomiting, heartburn, abdominal pain, indigestion, constipation, diarrhea, ulcers (gastric or duodenal), bleeding (in severe cases).
* **Cardiovascular:** Increased risk of heart attack, stroke, high blood pressure, and fluid retention.
* **Central Nervous System:** Dizziness, headache, drowsiness, confusion, tinnitus (ringing in the ears).
* **Renal:** Impaired kidney function.
* **Allergic reactions:** Skin rash, itching, hives, swelling (angioedema), difficulty breathing.
* **Other:**  Increased risk of bleeding, liver damage.

This is not an exhaustive list, and the severity and likelihood of these side effects can vary depending on individual factors and dosage.


**How it Works:**

Piroxicam is a nonsteroidal anti-inflammatory drug (NSAID) that works by inhibiting the production of prostaglandins, which are substances in the body that cause pain, inflammation, and fever. Beta-cyclodextrin acts as a solubilizer, improving the dissolution and absorption of piroxicam in the body, leading to better and more consistent drug levels in the bloodstream. This can improve efficacy and potentially reduce the need for higher doses, minimizing side effects associated with high piroxicam concentrations.
