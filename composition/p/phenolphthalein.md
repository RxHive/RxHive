# Phenolphthalein

**Generic Name:** Phenolphthalein

**Usage:**

* **Laxative:**  Phenolphthalein's primary use is as a stimulant laxative. It stimulates the intestines to increase bowel movements. * **Less Common but More Serious:**  Dehydration (due to prolonged diarrhea), electrolyte imbalance, allergic reactions (rash, itching, swelling), and in rare cases, changes in urine color (pink or red-brown).

The exact mechanism of this stimulation isn't fully understood but involves interfering with the balance of electrolytes in the intestinal cells.
