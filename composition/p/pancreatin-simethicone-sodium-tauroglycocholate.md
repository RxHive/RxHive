# Pancreatin + Simethicone + Sodium Tauroglycocholate

**Generic Name:** Pancreatin + Simethicone + Sodium Tauroglycocholate

**Usage:** This combination medication is typically used to treat symptoms of various digestive disorders characterized by insufficient pancreatic enzyme production and/or gas.  Specific conditions it might help include:

* **Exocrine pancreatic insufficiency (EPI):** A condition where the pancreas doesn't produce enough digestive enzymes.  This often leads to poor digestion of fats, proteins, and carbohydrates, resulting in symptoms like steatorrhea (fatty stools), abdominal pain, bloating, and weight loss.
* **Chronic pancreatitis:** Inflammation of the pancreas that can impair its ability to produce enzymes.
* **Cystic fibrosis:** A genetic disorder that affects multiple organs, including the pancreas.
* **Digestive discomfort with gas:** Simethicone helps to break up gas bubbles in the digestive tract, relieving bloating and discomfort.

**How it Works:**

* **Sodium Tauroglycocholate:** This is a bile salt that aids in the emulsification and absorption of fats.  It enhances the effectiveness of lipase in breaking down fats, particularly beneficial for patients with fat malabsorption.
