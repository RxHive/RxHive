# Paracetamol + Phenylpropanolamine + Chlorpheniramine Maleate + Sodium Citrate

**Generic Name:** Paracetamol + Phenylpropanolamine + Chlorpheniramine Maleate + Sodium Citrate

**Usage:** This combination drug is typically used to relieve symptoms of the common cold and flu.  Specifically, it targets:

* **Paracetamol (Acetaminophen):** Reduces fever (antipyretic) and relieves mild to moderate pain (analgesic).
* **Phenylpropanolamine:** Acts as a decongestant, shrinking swollen blood vessels in the nasal passages to relieve stuffiness.  **(Note: Phenylpropanolamine is largely discontinued in many countries due to concerns about cardiovascular side effects. Its presence in this combination likely indicates an older formulation or a product from a region where it's still allowed.)**
* **Chlorpheniramine Maleate:** Acts as an antihistamine, reducing allergy symptoms like runny nose, sneezing, and itchy eyes.
* **Sodium Citrate:** Acts as a buffering agent to help reduce stomach upset.


**Side Effects:**  Side effects can vary depending on the individual and the dosages of each ingredient.  Possible side effects include:

* **Paracetamol:**  Rarely, serious liver damage with overdose.  Nausea, vomiting (less common).
* **Phenylpropanolamine:** Increased blood pressure, palpitations, headache, nervousness, dizziness, insomnia, nausea, vomiting.  **(Serious cardiovascular side effects are a significant concern.)**
* **Chlorpheniramine Maleate:** Drowsiness, dry mouth, blurred vision, constipation, dizziness.  In some cases, urinary retention.
* **Sodium Citrate:** Usually well-tolerated, but can cause mild stomach upset in some individuals.


**How it Works:**

* **Paracetamol:**  Reduces fever by acting on the hypothalamus region of the brain, which regulates body temperature. Its analgesic effect is not fully understood but is thought to involve inhibition of prostaglandin synthesis.
* **Phenylpropanolamine:**  Causes vasoconstriction (narrowing of blood vessels) in the nasal passages, reducing swelling and congestion.
* **Chlorpheniramine Maleate:** Blocks the action of histamine, a chemical released by the body during allergic reactions.  This reduces allergy symptoms.
* **Sodium Citrate:**  Increases the pH of the solution, making it less acidic and reducing the likelihood of stomach irritation.
