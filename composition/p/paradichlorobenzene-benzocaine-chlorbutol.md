### Side Effects

**Because this is not a recognized pharmaceutical combination, a comprehensive list of side effects is not possible.** However, potential adverse effects based on the individual components include:

* **Paradichlorobenzene:**  Skin irritation, eye irritation, respiratory irritation, nausea, vomiting, headache, dizziness, drowsiness, and in severe cases, liver and kidney damage.

* **Benzocaine:**  Allergic reactions (including methemoglobinemia, a serious blood disorder), skin irritation, and rarely, systemic effects (affects the whole body).

* **Chlorbutol:**  Skin irritation, allergic reactions, and potential for CNS depression (affecting the brain and nervous system).
