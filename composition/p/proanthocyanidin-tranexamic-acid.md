# Proanthocyanidin + Tranexamic Acid

* **Tranexamic Acid:** This is primarily used to reduce or prevent excessive bleeding, often after surgery or in conditions like heavy menstrual bleeding (menorrhagia).  It's a prescription medication.

* **Tranexamic Acid:** Works by inhibiting the breakdown of blood clots (fibrinolysis), thus reducing bleeding.
