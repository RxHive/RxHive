# Nimesulide + Metamizole

**Generic Name:** Nimesulide + Metamizole Sodium (combination drug)

* **Post-surgical pain:** Pain following surgery.
* **Dental pain:** Toothaches and other oral pain.
* **Menstrual pain:**  Dysmenorrhea.
* **Fever:**  To reduce high fever.
