**How it Works:** Vinorelbine is a vinca alkaloid antineoplastic agent.  It works by interfering with the cell's ability to divide and replicate. Specifically, it binds to tubulin, a protein that's crucial for the formation of microtubules. Microtubules are essential components of the cell's cytoskeleton and play a vital role in cell division. By disrupting microtubule function, vinorelbine prevents cancer cells from dividing and ultimately leads to their death.
