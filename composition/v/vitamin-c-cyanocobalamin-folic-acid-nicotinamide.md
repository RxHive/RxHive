* **Nicotinamide (B3):**  Flushing (redness of the skin), liver damage (with very high doses), gastrointestinal upset.


**How it Works:** Each component in this combination supplement plays a vital role in the body:

* **Vitamin C (Ascorbic Acid):**  A powerful antioxidant, crucial for immune function, collagen synthesis (important for skin, bones, and connective tissue), and iron absorption.
* **Cyanocobalamin (Vitamin B12):** Essential for nerve function, red blood cell formation, and DNA synthesis.  A deficiency can lead to pernicious anemia and neurological problems.
* **Folic Acid (Vitamin B9):** Crucial for cell growth and development, especially important during pregnancy to prevent neural tube defects.  Involved in DNA and RNA synthesis.
* **Nicotinamide (Vitamin B3):** Plays a vital role in energy metabolism, DNA repair, and cell signaling.  Contributes to healthy skin and nervous system function.
