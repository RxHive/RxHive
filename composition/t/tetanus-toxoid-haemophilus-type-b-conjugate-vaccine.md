**How it Works:**

* **Tetanus Toxoid:** The tetanus toxoid component is an inactivated (killed) form of the tetanus toxin.  It stimulates the body's immune system to produce antibodies against the toxin, protecting against tetanus (lockjaw).  It does *not* provide protection against the bacteria itself, only the powerful toxin it produces.
* **Haemophilus influenzae type b (Hib) Conjugate:** The Hib component is a conjugate vaccine, meaning the Hib bacteria's polysaccharide capsule (which is poorly immunogenic on its own) is attached to a protein carrier. This conjugation enhances the immune response, leading to the production of antibodies against Hib bacteria.  This protection is against invasive Hib disease, such as meningitis and pneumonia, caused by the bacteria.
