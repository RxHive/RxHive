* Muscle spasms:  Thiocolchicoside specifically targets muscle spasms, providing muscle relaxation.
* Inflammation: Diclofenac is a potent anti-inflammatory drug, reducing swelling and pain associated with inflammation.

**How it Works:**

* **Thiocolchicoside:** This drug acts as a muscle relaxant. Its exact mechanism isn't fully understood, but it's thought to interfere with nerve impulses that cause muscle spasms, leading to relaxation.
* **Diclofenac:** This is a nonsteroidal anti-inflammatory drug (NSAID). It inhibits the production of prostaglandins, chemicals in the body that cause pain, inflammation, and fever. By reducing prostaglandin levels, diclofenac decreases pain and inflammation.
