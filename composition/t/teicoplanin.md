# Teicoplanin

**Generic Name:** Teicoplanin

**Usage:** Teicoplanin is a glycopeptide antibiotic used to treat serious bacterial infections, particularly those caused by Gram-positive bacteria that are resistant to other antibiotics.  It's often reserved for severe infections where other treatments have failed or are unsuitable.  Specific uses include:

* Severe skin and skin structure infections (e.g., cellulitis, abscesses)
* Bacterial endocarditis (infection of the heart valves)
* Pneumonia (lung infection)
* Intra-abdominal infections (infections within the abdomen)
* Bone and joint infections (osteomyelitis)
* Sepsis (a life-threatening condition caused by the body's overwhelming response to infection)
