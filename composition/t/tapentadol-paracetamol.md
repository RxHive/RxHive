# Tapentadol + Paracetamol

**Generic Name:** Tapentadol + Paracetamol (also known as Tapentadol/Paracetamol or Tapentadol/Acetaminophen)

**How it Works:**

* **Tapentadol:** This is a synthetic opioid analgesic.
