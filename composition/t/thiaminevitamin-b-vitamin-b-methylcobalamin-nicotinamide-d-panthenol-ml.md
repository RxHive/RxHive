* **Thiamine (Vitamin B1) deficiency:** This can lead to conditions like beriberi (affecting the nervous system and heart) and Wernicke-Korsakoff syndrome (primarily affecting the brain).

### How it Works

Each vitamin in this combination has its own specific role:

* **Thiamine (B1):**  Essential coenzyme in carbohydrate metabolism, crucial for energy production and nerve function.

* **D-Panthenol (Provitamin B5):** A precursor to pantothenic acid (B5), involved in energy production and the synthesis of hormones and neurotransmitters.
* **Methylcobalamin:** A form of Vitamin B12, crucial for nerve function, red blood cell formation, and DNA synthesis.


By supplementing these vitamins, the body is provided with the necessary co-factors to support these vital metabolic processes.
