**1. Trihexyphenidyl:**

* **Usage:** Primarily used to treat Parkinson's disease symptoms (tremors, rigidity, and muscle stiffness) and drug-induced parkinsonism (symptoms caused by antipsychotic medication). It's an anticholinergic.
