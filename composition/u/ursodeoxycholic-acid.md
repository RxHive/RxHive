# Ursodeoxycholic Acid

**Generic Name:** Ursodeoxycholic acid (UDCA)

**Usage:**

Ursodeoxycholic acid is primarily used to treat:

* **Gallstones:**  Specifically, it's used to dissolve cholesterol gallstones in the gallbladder.  It's most effective in patients with small, radiolucent (not visible on X-ray) stones and a functioning gallbladder.  It's not effective for all gallstone types.
* **Primary biliary cholangitis (PBC):**  It helps slow the progression of this liver disease by reducing the damage to bile ducts.
* **Primary sclerosing cholangitis (PSC):**  While not as effective as in PBC, it may help some patients with PSC.
* **Chronic liver disease:** It can be used in certain types of chronic liver diseases, to improve liver function.
* **Cystic fibrosis related liver disease:** UDCA may be used to help manage liver disease in patients with cystic fibrosis.
* **Other Liver diseases:** In some cases, it can help with other forms of liver disease.
