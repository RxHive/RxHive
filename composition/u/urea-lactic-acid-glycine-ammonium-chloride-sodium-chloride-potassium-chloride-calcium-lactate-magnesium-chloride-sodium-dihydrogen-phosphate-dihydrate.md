### Usage

This combined solution is primarily used to treat or prevent fluid and electrolyte imbalances. This can be necessary in various situations, including:

* **Dehydration:**  Replenishing fluids lost due to vomiting, diarrhea, excessive sweating, or other causes.

### Side Effects

Common side effects associated with IV electrolyte solutions are generally related to the administration itself or to rapid fluid shifts.  These can include:

* **Phlebitis:** Inflammation of the vein at the IV insertion site.
* **Fluid overload:**  If administered too quickly, excess fluid can overload the circulatory system. Symptoms may include shortness of breath, swelling in the extremities, and increased blood pressure.
* **Electrolyte imbalances:** While intended to correct imbalances, improper dosing can lead to other electrolyte disturbances.  This is monitored through blood tests.

Less common but more serious side effects are possible, especially with improper administration. These should be reported immediately to a healthcare professional.
